France, and its impact on what it means to be an American


  America has been Influenced by many countries around the world, each leaving a little bit of their culture with us. The French were a main contributor in helping shape America’s culture, and its history along the way. They sold us over a third of our country (The Loisiana Purcase in 1803), and they even helped us with training, and weapons to sucseed with our revolution. We owe our existence to them, and as a result, it is only natural that we carry a little bit of them with us. One of the more noticeable things today that the French have added to the culture of the United States was its food. While most foods we define as “French” have been moderately Americanized,
we can still see that they are french at its start, and that the level of Deliciousness is the same. Some of the foods brought over from the French include croissants, eclairs, crepes, cream puffs, souffles, and even cinnamon buns! Another thing that the French added to the culture of America is their willingness to fight the British and help us, the Americans fight for our independence. Fashion is perhaps the biggest thing we can associate the French with! Some of the most “in”, and “out” clothes are found right in the fashion shows hosted by the US, France, and the UK! Even perfumes are said to be French! All o that said, France is one of the greatest influences on what makes America what it is today!
